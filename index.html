Hydroponic systems

Systems designed for farms, restaurants, and large-scale operations.

  • NFT (Nutrient Film Technique) — Continuous flow along channels; ideal for leafy greens and herbs.
  • Deep Water Culture (DWC) — Roots suspended in oxygenated nutrient solution; high yield, scalable.
  • Ebb & Flow — Flood and drain cycles; flexible for a wide range of crops.
  • Vertical farming systems — Stacked tiers to maximize space and output per square foot.
  • Greenhouse integration — Climate-controlled setups with hydroponics for year-round production.
  • Modular rack systems — Customizable layouts for warehouses and indoor farms.

Compact systems for homes, kitchens, and offices.

  • Countertop herb gardens — Small units for herbs and microgreens on your kitchen counter.
  • Vertical wall units — Space-saving panels for indoor walls or balconies.
  • Compact DWC — Single or multi-pod systems for salads and herbs.
  • Kratky method units — Low-maintenance, passive systems—no pumps required.
  • Smart indoor gardens — LED-lit, app-connected units for consistent growth.
  • Desktop & shelf systems — Stylish grow stations for home or office.
